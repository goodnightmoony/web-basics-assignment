About Me

Hello! I'm Delia, and my pronouns are she/her. I am a 25 year old grad student at Pratt Institute, currently working on getting my Masters of Library and Information Science. I was born and raised in southeastern Connecticut, where I spent a lot of time out doors growing up, whether that be at the beach, in the woods, or at the park. I have always been a bookworm, fostered by my late grandmother who encouraged me to read "the classics," as she put it. All that time spent in books, bookstores, and libraries, planted the seed of my little dream to become a librarian. As an adult, I have come thoroughly value the communal space of learning and knowledge sharing that libraries can be. It is my dream to help foster that flow of information in whatever way I can as a librarian.
